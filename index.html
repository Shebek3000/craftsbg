Preserving the tradition and spirit of Bulgarian heritage

Pottery

Bulgarian pottery has a long-standing tradition with distinctive styles and colorful ornaments. The town of Troyan is especially famous for its ceramics, known as "Troyan pottery."

Woodcarving

Woodcarving is a traditional craft in Bulgaria, seen in monastery iconostases, traditional homes, and folk instruments. The town of Tryavna is a historic center for master woodcarvers.

Weaving and Embroidery

Bulgarian women have long practiced weaving colorful rugs (kilimi) and creating intricate embroidery that decorates traditional costumes and household textiles.

Icon Painting

Iconography is a sacred art in Bulgaria, especially flourishing during the Bulgarian National Revival period. Tryavna and Samokov were notable centers for icon painters.
